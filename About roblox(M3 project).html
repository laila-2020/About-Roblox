1-The best games


Some players dont prefer some games that are very loved by other players . Also some of these games aren't trending now but these games are the best games that have been made until now in Roblox . From these games:

1-Brookhaven RP: This role-playing game allows players to live out their dream lives in a virtual city, offering a relaxing and creative experience with customizable houses, jobs, and more, according to EGamersWorld.

2-Blox Fruits: Inspired by the popular anime One Piece, this game allows players to become powerful swordsmen or Blox Fruit users, exploring the seas, battling enemies, and striving to become the strongest

3-Adopt Me!: Originally a role-playing game focusing on parenting and adoption, Adopt Me! has evolved to emphasize pet adoption, care, and trading, and also offers a gamepass for building custom houses, according to Online Tech Tips.

4-Grow a Garden: A popular farming simulator where players plant seeds, grow crops, and harvest them for profit, says PCGamesN.

5-Pet Simulator X: A game where players collect and train pets, exploring different worlds and hatching new eggs.

6-Murder Mystery 2: A popular game where players try to solve murders, with one player being the murderer, one the sheriff, and the rest innocents.

7-The Strongest Battlegrounds: A fighting game where players can train and battle using various fighting styles and abilities.
